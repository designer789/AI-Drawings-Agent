Features

Drawing Animation

Upload your own drawings (JPEG/PNG), and watch as they are animated with fluid movements and engaging actions. Perfect for creating personalized animations without needing technical skills.

Pose Recognition

The agent detects the pose and structure of your character, building a skeletal framework that allows for realistic motion.

Pre-set Motion Templates

Choose from a variety of built-in motion templates—walking, waving, jumping, or even dancing. Tweak and adjust animations directly using our interactive interface. Define movements, set timing, and make adjustments in real-time.

Export Options

Save your animations as MP4, GIF, or even interactive web files to share on social media, integrate into presentations, or use in educational content.

Material Library

Access a wide range of pre-built characters, objects, and backgrounds to kickstart your animations.

TGBot Functionality & GitHub Integration

Easily create animations with a few simple commands through Telegram Bot. Leverage GitHub open-source code to customize your animations locally or process them in bulk.

How to Use

AI Drawings Agent uses state-of-the-art AI models to analyze and animate your drawings. Here's how it works:

1

Access Telegram Bot

Search for @AI_DrawingsBot on Telegram and click "Start."

2

Choose Material or Upload Your Drawing

Use the command /materials to browse available assets.

Or upload your custom drawing (JPEG/PNG format).

3

Generate Animation

Enter /generate and choose an action template (e.g., "walk," "wave," or "jump").

The Bot will process your request and generate an animation preview.

4

Download & Share

Once generated, the Bot provides a download link for MP4 or GIF format.

Share your animations instantly on social media!
